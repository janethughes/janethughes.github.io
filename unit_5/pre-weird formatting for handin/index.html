Janet Hughes

Digital strategy, leadership and delivery

Strategy

I help leadership teams and organisations identify and resolve the most important strategic questions and issues facing them in the digital age.

Recent clients include Government Digital Service and NHS Digital.

Leadership

I provide coaching and training for senior leaders.

I mentor people, both informally and through formal schemes such as Ambitious Ladies in Tech.

I informally advise leaders and organisations that are trying to make things work better for people, including Big Lottery Fund and DXW.

Delivery

I've acted as programme or product director for complex projects and programmes, including 4 years at the Government Digital Service.

I provide short-term interventions to help diagnose and solve problems and blockers to swift, effective delivery of digital projects and programmes. Recent clients include the Royal Academy of Arts and the Open Data Institute.

I help organise and run events like UK Govcamp and Homelesshack.

Writing and speaking

I write and talk on a range of topics based on my experience, research and practise.

  • Digital leadership

    I write on Medium for Doteveryone about effective leadership in a digital age and what it means to be a digital organisation

  • Delivery

    I believe in working in the open, so I usually (with clients' permission) like to write about the work I'm doing with organisations, for example on NHS Digital's first unconference, or how goals, cycles and people interact at the Government Digital Service

  • Digital government

    I write and take part in panel debates about government in a digital age - see for example my article on boldness in the civil service, and appearing on a panel at the Institute for Government
